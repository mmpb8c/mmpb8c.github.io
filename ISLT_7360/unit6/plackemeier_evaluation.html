Another weakness when reviewing the evaluations was that I need a specific area on the home page that explains exactly why I made this website. I think putting this on the home screen will help the visitors better understand the exact purpose behind my website and what motivated me to create this blog!

Recommended/Planned Changes to the Design

A few of the changes that I plan on making to my website are:

General Layout of my website

This is an area in which I recieved most of my feedback. I realize how important the layout of your website can be. It can sometimes be the make or break factor of a site. With that being said, I will make some changes to the general layout of my website. Both my sister and one of my classmates, Yixiang Gao suggest that I make sure that the layout of my entire website is consistent. Having all pages of your website organized and looking the same makes it way easier for those visiting your website to find what they are looking for much faster and easier. Specifically, I will be moving the photo on my Health and Fitness Page to the left to match the home page!

Purpose of the Website

According to the evaluations, one thing that I need to work on is defining and explaining the purpose of my website to the viewers. In order to fix this, I will put a paragraph on my home page explaining the purpose and driving force behind my website and why I chose to create this blog.

Changes to Navigation

When reviewing the feedback that I recieved from both my classmates and evaluators, Navigation was another aspect that I was told to work on. My cousin Katie mentioned that she can't tell what page she was on when going through my website. As I realize that this is important to have for your visitors, I plan to create a way in which viewers know what page they are currently on.

Reflection on Evaluation Process

I personally have really enjoyed this part of the course. The evaluation process is very important because it helps ensure that your website is as effective as it should be. This process helps you identify the areas in which your website needs improvement and ultimately helps you realize your goals more effeciently. Having both my classmates and others outside of the class evaluate my website allows me to learn from not only my mistakes but also learn from the mistakes of others. This process has definitely been very beneficial to me because it has made me realize the strengths and weaknesses of my site from people using it on the other end. I believe that nothing is more useful that others honest feedback. I personally chose three people whom don't have much experience with web development and coding because it let me know the outlook of my website from people who would randomly be using it whereas the evaluations made from my classmates helped me more with the layout and overall design of my website. So, this allowed me to have my different perspectives and types of feeback which I really appreciate and find very helpful.
